Frequently asked questions

WHAT PLATFORM DO YOU BUILD THE WEBSITES ON?

I specialize in developing websites primarily using **WordPress**, as it provides the perfect balance of custom design flexibility and user-friendly content management for my clients. For simple, static sites, I may use pure HTML/CSS/JS or modern frameworks like React, depending on the client's needs.

HOW MUCH WILL MY WEBSITE COST?

Since every website is custom-designed, pricing is tailored to your specific goals and complexity. My general project range starts at $5,000 for foundational sites and increases based on features like e-commerce, complex integrations, and advanced functionality. Please schedule a call for a personalized quote.

WILL MY NEW WEBSITE BE MOBILE FRIENDLY?

Absolutely. Every website I build is designed using a **mobile-first approach**. This ensures your site looks and functions perfectly on all screen sizes, from the smallest smartphones to large desktop monitors, which is critical for modern SEO and user experience.

WILL MY NEW WEBSITE BE OPTIMIZED FOR PAGE SPEED?

Yes. I employ modern optimization techniques, including efficient code, image compression, and intelligent asset loading, to ensure fast load times. Speed is vital for retaining visitors and achieving high search engine rankings.

HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO BUILD MY WEBSITE?

The average timeline for a custom website from start to launch is typically **6 to 8 weeks**, depending on the project scope and how quickly content and feedback are provided by the client. We will establish a clear timeline at the start of our project.

WHAT IF I NEED TO UPDATE MY SITE'S CONTENT AFTER IT'S LAUNCHED?

I build all sites with a user-friendly CMS (like WordPress) and provide comprehensive training. You will be fully equipped to update text, add images, and manage basic content yourself. For larger structural changes, I offer ongoing support packages.

WHAT ABOUT SEO?

I ensure your website is built with **on-page SEO best practices**—including proper structure, metadata implementation, fast loading, and mobile responsiveness—to give you a strong foundation for search engine visibility from day one.
